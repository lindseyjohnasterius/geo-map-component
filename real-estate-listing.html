Hospitals & Opportunity Zones

Of the 8,762 census tracts across the county that have been designated as Opportunity Zones, 2,905 (33%) either contain a hospital or are ½ a mile from a hospital. Compensation for healthcare providers is increasing and hospitals are staffing up with travel nurses.

Garfield County Opportunity Zone

Garfield County is committed to deploying an array of local, state and federal incentives to attract potential investors. For example, Yogurtland recently came to Panguitch with the help of a Rural Fast Track Grant. This effort included a 50/50 fund match up to $50,000 of the total project cost of $105,000. This retail establishment created new jobs that pay over 110% of the average wage in the area, which demonstrates a success for employees, their families, and the local economy.

At present, Garfield County is committed to leveraging the following incentives to support project development and delivery:

  • Rural Economic Development Incentive (REDI) Grant
  • Business Expansion and Retention (BEAR)
  • Rural Online Initiative (ROI)
  • Rural Coworking and Innovation Center (RCIC) Grant
  • Utah Rural Jobs Program
  • Targeted Business Tax Credits
  • Rural Rapid Manufacturing Grant
