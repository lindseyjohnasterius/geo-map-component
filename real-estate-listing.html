Hospitals & Opportunity Zones Of the 8,762 census tracts across the county that have been designated as Opportunity Zones, 2,905 (33%) either contain a hospital or are ½ a mile from a hospital. Compensation for healthcare providers is increasing and hospitals are staffing up with travel nurses. Locums doctors and travel nurses often stay in substandard hotels and short term rentals because they don’t have alternatives, especially when working in the small hospital towns of the rural mountain west. Our proposed portfolio identifies multifamily or hotel properties in attractive opportunity zones that can accommodate healthcare providers as 2-16 week rentals with shared kitchens and other amenities these renters want.
